Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj: A Legacy of Courage and Vision

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, a towering figure in Indian history, was established the Swarajya in the 17th century.His life and legacy continue to resonate through the ages.

Born in 1630 at the Shivneri Fort near Pune, Shivaji was brought up in a family steeped in Maratha traditions and values. Under the guidance of his mother, Jijabai, he received a rigorous education in both academics and martial arts. From a young age, Shivaji exhibited exceptional intelligence, courage, and a deep sense of patriotism.
